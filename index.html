01 — About

The Man
Behind VIO

Faith, craft, and relentless curiosity are the three pillars of everything I build.

My name is Victor Igbaji Ogabor, a freelance web and app developer, graphic designer, and writer operating under my personal brand, VIO. My core focus is web and application development, where I design and build functional, scalable digital products. Alongside this, I bring strong capabilities in graphic design and writing to create well-rounded, user-centered solutions.

I am currently a Statistics student at the University of Benin, Nigeria, where my interest in data and systems shapes how I approach problem-solving, product development, and business thinking. I am self-taught in modern web technologies, with hands-on experience in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and backend development using Node.js, Express.js, and related tools within the JavaScript ecosystem. This technical foundation allows me to move from concept to execution efficiently, building solutions that are both practical and performance-driven.

Across real products I have built and shipped, I have worked with React, Next.js, Firebase, Supabase, PostgreSQL with PostGIS, Expo React Native, and GitHub Actions for CI and automated APK pipelines. I have deployed on Vercel, Cloudflare Pages, Render, and Koyeb, and integrated third-party services including the Groq API and Twilio to build AI-powered tools and WhatsApp automation. Every product I have shipped reflects the same commitment: clean architecture, real-world utility, and intentional design from the first line of code to the final interface.

I am a committed Christian, and my faith guides my values, discipline, and the intention behind my work. I am focused on building purposeful solutions, delivering real value, and creating a lasting impact through everything I do. I am not just writing code. I am building a legacy with purpose at the center.
