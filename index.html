01 — About

The Man
Behind VIO

Faith, craft, and relentless curiosity are the three pillars of everything I build.

My name is Victor Igbaji Ogabor, a freelance web and app developer, graphic designer, and writer operating under my personal brand, VIO. My core focus is web and application development, where I design and build functional, scalable digital products.

I am currently a Statistics student at the University of Benin, Nigeria, where my interest in data and systems shapes how I approach problem-solving, product development, and business thinking. I am self-taught in modern web technologies, with hands-on experience in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and backend development using Node.js, Express.js, and related tools within the JavaScript ecosystem.

Across real products I have built and shipped, I have worked with React, Next.js, Firebase, Supabase, PostgreSQL with PostGIS, Expo React Native, and GitHub Actions for CI and automated APK pipelines. I have deployed on Vercel, Cloudflare Pages, Render, and Koyeb, and integrated third-party services including the Groq API and Twilio to build AI-powered tools and WhatsApp automation.

I am a committed Christian, and my faith guides my values, discipline, and the intention behind my work. I am not just writing code. I am building a legacy with purpose at the center.
