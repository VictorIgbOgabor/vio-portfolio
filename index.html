About

The Man Behind VIO

Faith, craft, and relentless curiosity are the three pillars of everything I build.

My name is Victor Igbaji Ogabor, a freelance web and app developer, graphic designer, and writer operating under my personal brand, VIO.

I am currently a Statistics student at the University of Benin, Nigeria, where my interest in data and systems shapes how I approach problem-solving and product development. I am self-taught in modern web technologies — HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Node.js, React, Next.js, Firebase, Supabase, PostgreSQL, and more.

Across real products I have shipped, I have worked with Expo React Native, GitHub Actions, Vercel, Cloudflare, Groq API, and Twilio to build AI-powered tools and WhatsApp automation.

I am a committed Christian. My faith guides my values, discipline, and the intention behind every line of code I write. I am not just building software. I am building a legacy.
