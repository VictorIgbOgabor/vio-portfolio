FAQ

Common Questions

Do you work with clients outside Nigeria?
Absolutely. Most of my clients are international. I work remotely with teams and individuals across Africa, Europe, North America, and beyond. Communication is async-friendly.
How does the revision process work?
Basic gets 2 rounds, Standard gets 5 rounds, and Premium gets unlimited revisions until you are 100% satisfied. Revisions are structured per milestone, not open-ended.
Can I pay in installments?
Yes. The standard arrangement is 50% upfront to begin and 50% on delivery. For larger projects we can discuss a milestone-based payment schedule.
What if I need something not listed here?
Just reach out. I handle a wide range of digital work and I am open to custom scopes, retainer arrangements, and project-based collaborations not covered by standard packages.
How do we communicate during the project?
Primarily via WhatsApp or email. I send progress updates at key milestones and am responsive during business hours across most time zones.
Do you offer ongoing maintenance?
Yes. After the included support period, I offer monthly maintenance retainers for updates, security, and improvements. Pricing depends on the scope required.
